4.0 Payment Process

Payment records follow a scheme that allows easy reporting. Payments made on behalf of a student are recorded, but they are organized according to school, programme unit, financial year and other attributes. At the beginning of the financial year, the account office closes the current financial year record and opens a new financial year record.  A payment request is initiated by programme unit field officers to request payment of scholarship to a group of students. The field officer creates a payment request record, and adds verified sponsored students from the programme unit to this request. Once ready, the request is submitted to accounts electronically. A payment request submitted can not be edited by field officer. To make any change the account office has to “return” the request by changing its status.

 

The payment records follow the following hierarchical structure

Financial Year à Payment Request from programme area à Payment for one student for an academic year

 

4.1 Create a Financial Year

  1. Select from the menu Payments à  Financial Support data à Financial Year
  2. Click add
  3. Enter a descriptive name, start date and end date.
  4. Click the add button

4.2 Create Student Verification List

  1. Select Sponsored Programme Unit à  By School from the menu.
  2. Select the school which you like to create a verification list for.
  3. A list of student in that school opens
  4. Export the list to excel.
  5. Format the list into sign up sheet. 

4.2 Create Payment Request

  1. Select Paymentsà All Payments Request from the menu
  2. Click on the add link
  3. Enter Payment Request Name.  The name should be a name that will make it easy to refer to the record. For example a combination of the month year programme unit/school
  4. Click Add

4.3 Add students to Payment Request

  1. Select Payments àNew Payment Requests from the menu
  2. Select the correct Payment Request from the list and click on Select Students for Payment link.
  3. A sponsored students lists within the programme unit opens
  4. Find the record of the student verified to be attending school in this list
  5. Click the Add link along the name of the student to add the student to the current payment request record. 
  6. To remove a student for payment request, click on remove link.
  7. To check if the student is added to the request or not click on status link

 

 

 

Note: Adding a student to the payment request creates a payment record for the student based on the scholarship awarded. The total amount of the request is calculated based on this record.

4.5 Submit Payment Request

After compiling a payment request a field officer has to submit the request so that finance officers can access it. Once a payment is submitted it can not be changed.

 

  1. Open all payment requests list
  2. Next to new payment request record there is a link to submit the request. Click on this link.
  3. A confirmation messages pops up
  4. Confirm submission by clicking ok button or cancel by clicking cancel button.

4.4 Disburse Payment

The finance office receives and disburses payment requests after satisfying themselves that the data is adequate and valid, based on among other things the verification documents supplied at the time of submitting the payment request.

To disburse a payment request, go to

  1. PaymentàDisburse Payment. All payment requests that have been submitted will be listed.
  2. To edit/approve of a payment request that is in the requested state, click on the “Edit” link. Alternatively, you can click on View link, the click on the “Edit” link from there
  3. Click on “Disbursed” radio button if you approve.
  4. Click on “Edit” button.
  5. Click “Confirm”

4.5 Liquidate Payment

The field officer receives and liquidates disbursed payments. To liquidate a payment request, go to

  1. PaymentàLiquidate Payment. All pending disbursed payments that have been submitted will be listed.
  2. To liquidate payment that is in the disbursed state, click on the “Edit” link. Alternatively, you can click on View link, the click on the “Edit” link from there
  3. Click on “Liquidate” radio button if you approve.
  4. provide a proof of the payment. This can be a scanned document/cheque or other valid proof. This is a required field.
  5. Click on “Edit” button.
  6. Click “Confirm”

 

4.6 Record Refund

To enter refund data

  1. Go to PaymentsàPayment Refund
  2. Select the school or paying program area or program area of residence of the student.
  3. Click search to show a list of matching records.
  4. Identify the student from the list, click on “Payment Refund” for that student.
  5. Click to Edit the detail record
  6. Enter the refund amount
  7. Click “Edit” followed by “Confirm” on the subsequent form
